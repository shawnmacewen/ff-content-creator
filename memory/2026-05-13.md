## Important debugging discoveries

- Provider payload shape includes `data.records` and keys such as `articleId`, `article_url`, `extra_properties`, `compliance_status`.
- A published/effective date can vary by response and may only appear in article detail responses for some items.
- “Showing 20 of 40” can be pagination display, while sync “updated” count can be higher due to updates to existing rows.
